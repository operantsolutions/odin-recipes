Breakfast Sandwich

Delciousness from Savannah

We first found this sandwich in Savannah at Cafe M.

However, we might like our homemade version even better!

Ingredients

  • Baguette
  • Butter
  • Bacon
  • Mozzarella
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper while warming the bread in the oven.
  2. Cook bacon using bacon weight.
  3. Slice bread and butter. Fill bread with crumbled bacon and eggs. Top with mozzarella slices.
  4. Broil on "low" on the bottom rack for two minutes.
  5. Serve.
