Pancakes

Fat Stacks of Flap Jacks

I sincerely hope you already know what pancakes are.

If not, they are fluffy flat disks of batter fried in a skillet.

Ingredients

  • Complete pancake mix
  • Water

Steps

  1. Whisk together pancake mix and water.
  2. Rest mixture two minutes (or until thick).
  3. Pour batter into hot skillet.
  4. Cook until edges set, then flip. Cook for 45 seconds.
  5. Serve.
